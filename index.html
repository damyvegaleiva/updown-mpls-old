The next generation of arcade bars has arrived to Minneapolis.

Up-Down Minneapolis features more than 50 arcade games from the ‘80s and ‘90s, pinball machines, four classic skee-ball alleys, bubble hockey, Nintendo 64 console gaming, life-size Jenga and Connect Four. All games cost just 25 cents.

Our drink menu includes an extensive craft beer selection with over 60 beers on tap and even more bottles and cans. We also feature a full bar and 4 cocktails on tap.

Up-Down has house-made pizza by the slice or you can also get a whole one with unique toppings.

"One of the best places in town to hangout with friends." Photo by Freddy G

At Up-Down we like to have fun and we want to make sure that you too, that's why every month we have fun events including: tournaments with prizes, costume contests, ROKKR watch party and more. Make sure to check our events section so you don't miss any of these.

Looking for a place to do your company's employee party? Book it at Up-Down and have a unique experience, reach out to us on our contact section for more info & details.

We are a pet-friendly place where you can bring your best friend withyou. We have a front patio, a back patio and a full size rooftop area for you to bring and have a good time with your pet too.
